Operation

The Adapter alerts you when you forget to connect the battery charger to your vehicle.

It discovers the garage when the signal of the base station is above the configured level (see chapter Configuration ).

It detects if the battery charger is connected by comparing the vehicle's battery voltage with the configured level (again, see chapter Configuration ).

Before the alert, which is quite annoying, there is a warning. Both alert and warning can be snoozed by touching the adapter.

Power on

On power on the adapter cycles colors on the LED and chimes a happy tune.

Garage detected

When the adapter detects the garage it plays two short beeps and one long beep.

Warning

When the vehicle is in the garage but is not charging the adapter will give you a warning.

The LED will slowly flash yellow and there is a short beep every five seconds.

Alert

After the warning you are alerted by a fast flashing red LED and an almost constant tone. It really is annoying.

Snooze

You can snooze the warning and the alert by touching the adapter for a few seconds. The LED will flash orange as long as the adapter is snoozing. It will resume to warn or alert you, whatever you interrupted.

Charging

The LED flashes slowly in a comforting green when the vehicle is in the garage and charging.

When the adapter detects the battery charger it beeps three times short and two times long.

Turn on Bluetooth

Touch the adapter for a few seconds until there is a low pitched beep to turn on Bluetooth.

The adapter will play two short and one long beep when it turns Bluetooth on.

You can turn Bluetooth off in the same fashion. The adapter will confirm it turned off Bluetooth with one long and two short tones.

This does not work if the adapter is already warning or alerting you.
