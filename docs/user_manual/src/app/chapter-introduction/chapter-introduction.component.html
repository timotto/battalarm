Introduction

The Battery Alarm adapter is meant to solve a specific problem.

We all know it and it has happened to everybody: you pick up your phone on your way out and the battery is almost dead because we forgot to plug it in.

The same can happen to power hungry vehicles: you receive an alert, jump into your truck and the starter doesn't even budge because you forgot to plug in the charging cable after your previous trip.
